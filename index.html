AgroSafe – Smart Animal Deterrent System

A futuristic 3D printed stationary robot designed to protect agricultural lands and gardens from animal disturbances using smart deterrent technologies.


About AgroSafe

AgroSafe is an innovative 3D printed robotic system that remains fixed in one location and protects crops from animals, especially peacocks and other field intruders.



V1: IoT-based laser robot that activates at night and rotates its head to deter animals.

V2: AI-based ESP32-CAM system that detects animals and activates water spraying.

V3: Combination of V1 and V2 mounted on a moving robot for full field coverage.

V4: Security system with ESP32-CAM for motor room protection and theft detection.
